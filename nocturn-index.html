Hidden gems

What the locals actually do

The places that don't make the listicles — and the ones that matter most.

Watts Towers

Iconic folk art towers in South LA — free on weekends. 17 interconnected spires built by one man over 34 years.

Go Sunday morning. You'll have it almost to yourself. Take the Blue Line to 103rd.

Barnsdall Art Park

Free hilltop park with a Frank Lloyd Wright house, a free gallery, and city views that beat Griffith without the crowds.

The gallery shows rotating local artists. The views at sunset are genuinely stunning.

Yucca's taco stand

James Beard Award-winning $4 tacos in Los Feliz. This unassuming stand is one of the most awarded in the country.

Cash only. The bean and cheese is all you need. Get three.

Angels Flight + Grand Central

Take the $1 funicular up Bunker Hill, eat a pupusa at Grand Central Market, walk back down. $6 total.

Operational since 1901 (with gaps). One of the world's shortest railways at 298 feet.

Brand Library — Glendale

A Moorish Revival castle that's actually a free public library and art gallery. Completely overlooked, completely magical.

Check out the art gallery on the upper floor. Local exhibitions, rotating every 6 weeks.

LA State Historic Park

32 acres of open space in the heart of downtown. Bring a blanket at sunset and watch the skyline turn gold.

Almost nobody knows about this park. Arrive 30 minutes before sunset for the best light.

Heritage Square Museum

Eight Victorian homes saved from demolition, clustered on a hill in Highland Park. LA's strangest outdoor museum.

Tours run weekends only. $10 gets you into all eight homes.

Echo Park Lake swan boats

$11/hr on a weekday morning. Glassy water, mist off the hills. Feels nothing like the rest of the city.

Arrive before 10am on a weekday. After noon it gets chaotic — mornings are meditative.

Hollyhock House courtyard

Frank Lloyd Wright's first Los Angeles commission sits in Barnsdall Park. The courtyard is free — just walk in.

The moat surrounding the building is still partially functional. Free to wander the grounds any time.
